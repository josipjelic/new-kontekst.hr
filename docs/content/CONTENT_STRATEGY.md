## Overview

Kontekst.hr is a specialized agency for business automation and AI solutions, aimed at business owners in Croatia who want to remove manual work and accelerate growth. We offer n8n workflow automation, business process automation, and bespoke AI applications. Core brand promise: your team freed from repetitive tasks, with technology working 24/7 on your behalf.

**Primary value proposition**: We replace hours of manual work with smart automations — your team focuses on growth; we handle the rest.

**Canonical brand statement (Croatian, live site)**: "Automatizacija poslovanja koja zaista radi."

## Brand Voice & Tone

### Voice (constant across all content)

| Dimension | Setting | Description |
|-----------|---------|-------------|
| Formality | Conversational, professional | Speak as a trusted peer to business owners — not stuffy, not casual. Approachable but competent. |
| Energy | Medium-to-high | Convey momentum and confidence. We don't hesitate; we act. Business owners are busy — copy that's slow or timid gets skipped. |
| Personality | Human, practical | We're people solving real problems, not a generic vendor. Mention Josip, the team's experience. Use "we" — it's honest. |
| Authority | Expert, earned | Don't oversell. Show results, methodology, and specifics (n8n, AI, workflow design). Confidence through clarity, not hype. |

### Tone by Context

| Context | Tone | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| Marketing headlines | Direct, benefit-led, no jargon | "Automatizacija poslovanja koja zaista radi" (emphasizes efficacy, not buzzwords) |
| Error messages | Calm, actionable, honest | "Previše pokušaja — čekajte 5 minuta prije novog pokušaja" (explains what happened and the fix) |
| Success confirmations | Warm, brief, forward-looking | "Hvala! Vaša poruka je poslana. Javit ćemo se uskoro." (thanks them, sets expectation) |
| Service descriptions | Specific, outcome-focused | "Zamjenjujemo ponavljajuće zadatke n8n workflowima koji rade 24/7" (what you get, not how we do it) |
| Process steps | Clear, sequential, confident | "Razumijevamo vaš posao" → "Gradimo automatizaciju" (no wishy-washy language) |
| CTA buttons | Conversational, action-oriented | "Razgovarajmo" or "Besplatni uvodni razgovor" (invites dialogue, no "Submit" or "Sign Up Now") |

### Voice Rules

- **Lead with the outcome, not the feature** — "Uštedite 20 sati rada tjedno" not "Koristi n8n za integracije"
- **Use "your" and "you"** — speak to the business owner directly: "Vaš tim se fokusira na rast" not "timovi se mogu fokusirati"
- **Be specific** — mention n8n by name, reference real use cases (workflow automation, CRM integration, AI apps) rather than generic "solutions"
- **Avoid jargon unless you explain it** — if we say "workflow," we show an example or an icon; if we say "AI," we explain what it does for them
- **Own the expertise** — we're not generic; we're Kontekst, we know automation, and we've done this before
- **Speak in active voice** — "We automate your processes" not "Processes can be automated by our team"

### Forbidden Phrases

- "Innovative" — overused, meaningless without context
- "Cutting-edge" — vague and often inaccurate (n8n is mature, proven, not bleeding-edge)
- "World-class" — claims without proof; say what we actually are
- "Synergies" or "synergy" — corporate jargon that alienates business owners
- "Leverage" as a verb (except in finance context) — awkward in Croatian
- "Reach out to us" — too informal; use "Razgovarajmo" or "Pošaljite nam poruku"
- Excessive exclamation marks — max one per page section; shows desperation

## Target Personas

> Personas are fully defined in `PRD.md` (read-only, owned by the product lead). This section captures copy-relevant insights for each to guide messaging.

### Persona 1: Jelena, the Operations Manager

**Company**: Mid-size e-commerce (15–40 people)

**Job-to-be-done**: Reduce the time her team spends on repetitive order entry, inventory checks, and data transfers between systems; give them back hours each day to focus on customer service or growth initiatives.

**Biggest objection**: "We've tried tools before and they were more trouble than they're worth. Will this actually work, or will it sit unused?" | "We're not very technical — can we understand how this works?"

**Language to use**: Tasks, processes, automation (not "infrastructure" or "APIs"); hours saved; team; efficiency; tools that "talk to each other"; same-day results

**Tone for this persona**: Practical, no-nonsense. She is busy. Show her exactly what will change and how much time she saves. Avoid long explanations.

**Primary CTA for this persona**: "Besplatni uvodni razgovor" — a live call where she can ask questions and see a concrete example relevant to her workflow

---

### Persona 2: Marko, the Business Owner

**Company**: Professional services firm (small to mid-size, freelancers up to 20 people)

**Job-to-be-done**: Scale his business without scaling his headcount. Delegate repetitive admin to software (invoicing reminders, client follow-ups, lead sorting) so he and his team can focus on billable, high-value work.

**Biggest objection**: "How much will this cost? Is it custom or a template?" | "What if it breaks and we lose our data?" | "Will this replace my team?"

**Language to use**: Scaling, growth, revenue per person, streamlined workflows, team focus, competitive advantage, integration (with his existing tools)

**Tone for this persona**: Confident, growth-oriented. He wants results and proof. Show ROI or clear before/after metrics. He respects expertise.

**Primary CTA for this persona**: "Pogledajte usluge" → detail services with specific examples, then "Razgovarajmo" to discuss his specific situation

---

### Persona 3: Nina, the Tech Lead (at a larger company)

**Company**: 50+ people, established business with legacy systems

**Job-to-be-done**: Modernize handoff between departments (sales to fulfillment, accounting to operations) without ripping out the current system. Build reliability and audit trails. Free her team from manual data munging.

**Biggest objection**: "Our systems are old/proprietary. Will n8n or custom integrations even work?" | "We need enterprise SLA, security, support." | "The cost of downtime is high."

**Language to use**: Integration, reliability, auditing, security, enterprise, SLA, specific platforms/tools (Salesforce, SAP, etc.); data integrity; compliance

**Tone for this persona**: Measured, detail-focused. She wants to understand architecture and risk. Show case studies of similar integrations. Be honest about what you can and can't do.

**Primary CTA for this persona**: "Razgovarajmo" (call) + offer a technical discovery call where she can ask about her specific stack and constraints
